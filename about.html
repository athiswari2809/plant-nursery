Who We Are

Welcome to Green Leaf Nursery. We started as a small backyard garden and have grown into one of the most trusted plant suppliers in the region. Our mission is to bring a piece of nature into every home and office through sustainable and organic practices.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, organic plants that improve indoor air quality and promote mental well-being for our urban communities.

Our Quality

Every plant is hand-nurtured by professional botanists and thoroughly checked for health before it ever reaches your door.
